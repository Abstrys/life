This applet is my Java implementation of John Horton Conway's now-classic "Game of Life".

There is quite an interesting page regarding its history at Wikipedia, including some intriguing patterns that produce rather bizarre behavior. You can, of course, try them out here!

Note: If you can't see the applet, it is likely you don't have the latest Java plugin installed.
Get it here.

 
Instructions
  • Click on any of the cells on the grid to toggle cells on or off.
  • To advance one generation, click on the Next button.
  • To clear the board and start over, click on the Clear button.
  • To continuously advance, click on the Animate button. This button will then change its name to Stop. Click it again to stop the animation.
